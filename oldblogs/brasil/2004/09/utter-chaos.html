Utter chaos

Hi all,

I made it safe to brasil. The plane from dusseldorf to frankfurt never got time to get any speed I guess since the flight took a whopping 30 minutes. Other than that the flights were reasonable eventless. Richard almost took the wrong bag from the plane but lucky for him there was another person with a similar bag walking towards us. He didn´t notice the exchange either untill they saw each other´s bag.

Martijn and Jenneke were there to pick us up from recife airport. After a long discussion with various taxi drivers we managed to get a taxi which didn´t state Airport with big letters. They didn´t want one, since we had to go into a shanty town, where they life, and they wanted to keep a reasonable low profile.
The shanty town were they life is not a real shanty town. There are brick houses, running water and electricity. The main reason why it is called a ´farvella´ by the government is because it lacks some things like decent roads.
The locals don´t think they´re in a farvella, the farvella´s are a few blocks away. And they are indeed shanty towns, with cardboard, plastic etc etc.

But we will move today to the beach :)
Time´s running out so another time more stories.

BTW the mobile phone works here so i might check it every now and then.
