Job
My supervisor asked to build an application called virtual scan. Basically it produces a 3D world out of a 2d bitmap where each cell is a landuse-type. The trial will be on a 200x200 bitmap, a whopping 40000cells! And each cell wil be put in 3d with 3DS Max models, which can run upto 20Mb each. We decided that somekind of optimalisation might bee useful.
Currently we are strugling to get the 3DS models nicely in virtools. The other thing is that I have yet to figure out a way to get the correct value of the cells. Virtools gives some ARGB colour which is of no use to me.

This all means that my thesis is slowed down a bit, not too bad since I've drawn most of the required diagrams on paper already.
